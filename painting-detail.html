"Bath" is a masterful exploration of serenity and structural contrast. Mike Kasongo captures a moment of suspended time, where the fluidity of water meets the geometric repetition of hair rollers. The subject, caught in a blissful state of repose, exists between the mundane preparation of beauty and a transcendent ethereal realm.

Kasongo's use of light creates a cinematic depth, with shadows that define the form with sculptural precision. The blue and grey palette of the environment accentuates the warmth of the subject's skin, making it the focal point of this emotive composition.

The Artist

Mike Kasongo

Based in Kinshasa, Kasongo's work is a dialogue between traditional African portraiture and contemporary surrealism. His pieces often feature everyday objects reimagined as crowns or architectural elements, elevating the daily rituals of life to high-art symbolism.
