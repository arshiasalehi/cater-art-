About the Artist

Mike Kasongo

Based in Kinshasa, Kasongo's work is a dialogue between traditional African portraiture and contemporary surrealism.

His pieces often feature everyday objects reimagined as crowns or architectural elements, elevating the daily rituals of life to high-art symbolism.

"Bath" is a masterful exploration of serenity and structural contrast, where fluidity meets the geometric repetition of domestic ritual.
